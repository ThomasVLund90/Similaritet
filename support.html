FAQ

Does the app upload my photos? No. Everything runs on your device.

Will it delete photos automatically? No. You always choose what to delete, and deleted items go to Recently Deleted (recoverable for 30 days).

Why is the first scan slow? Large iCloud libraries may need to download originals the first time; later scans only process new or changed photos.
