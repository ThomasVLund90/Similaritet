Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Similaritet ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy completely. The app runs entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any of your personal information.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect any personal data. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. No data ever leaves your device.

Access to your photos

With your permission, the app accesses your photo library to find visually similar photos. All processing — including image analysis using Apple's on-device Vision framework — happens locally on your device. Your photos and the index the app builds are never uploaded or sent anywhere.

On-device data

The app stores a local index (e.g. visual "feature prints" and basic metadata such as date and file size) on your device only, so that re-scans are faster. This index never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Deleting photos

When you choose to delete photos, the app uses Apple's standard Photos system, which moves them to "Recently Deleted." The app never deletes anything automatically. With iCloud Photos enabled, deletions sync via Apple's services according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
